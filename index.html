Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pot, add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cloves, cardamoms, and cinnamon stick.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  6. Add chicken and cook until it changes color.
  7. Stir in yogurt and garam masala, cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. In a separate pot, boil water and cook the soaked rice until 70% done. Drain the water.
  9. Layer the half-cooked rice over the chicken mixture, sprinkle fresh coriander and mint leaves.
  10. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes (Dum cooking method).
  11. Fluff the biryani gently and serve hot.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Add egg and vanilla extract, mix well.
  4. Stir in flour and baking soda.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
